Website Details

When you purchase a website from me, I take care of the full process so you can focus on your business. I handle planning, design, development, launch and the initial support after delivery. My goal is a fast, responsive site that's easy for you to update and that helps your customers find you.

What I normally do after a purchase

  • Discovery & planning — we define goals, pages, content and timeline.
  • Design & revisions — custom layout, brand alignment and up to 2 rounds of revisions (can be adjusted).
  • Development — responsive build, content entry (up to agreed pages), and basic SEO setup.
  • Testing & QA — cross-browser checks, performance and mobile testing.
  • Deployment — I help connect your domain and set up hosting or deploy to your chosen provider.
  • Handover & training — I provide brief documentation and a walkthrough so you can manage your site.
  • Support — 30 days of complimentary support after launch; optional ongoing maintenance plans are available.

Typical timeline: 2–6 weeks depending on scope. For a custom quote and timeline, contact me.
