Maintenance Details

Purchasing a maintenance plan means I take ongoing responsibility for keeping your site healthy, secure and up-to-date. I handle updates, backups, security checks, performance tweaks and small content edits so your site keeps working reliably.

What I normally do after a maintenance purchase

  • Initial audit & setup — I run a full site audit, configure backups, monitoring, and emergency access.
  • Updates & patching — regular CMS, plugin and dependency updates with compatibility checks.
  • Backups — scheduled off-site backups and verification so recovery is fast if needed.
  • Security & monitoring — malware scans, basic hardening and uptime/performance monitoring.
  • Content & small changes — minor content edits and image updates as agreed in the plan.
  • Emergency fixes — prioritized response for critical issues (response time depends on chosen plan).
  • Monthly reports — short summaries of updates, backups and any recommendations.

Plans are billed monthly or annually. The first month includes the setup audit and backup configuration. For plan details and pricing, get in touch and I’ll recommend the best option.
